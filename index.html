🚗 Getting In
Maytag Mountain Ranch Gate

Primary: 2013  ·  Alternate: 4350

Enter gate → Cowboy Way 1.5 mi → left on Running Bear Ln → 1.7 mi → first house on right.

Garage Access

Panel on right side of small garage. Code: 1952 then press Home.

Man door on right side will be unlocked — leads to mudroom. Park in the large bay.

Door Locking

Rotate handle up past horizontal until it stops, release to horizontal, then engage deadbolt. To unlock: reverse deadbolt, push handle down.

⚠ Always deadbolt before sleeping or leaving — wind will blow open unsecured doors.

🏊 Plunge Pool

Back patio facing Pikes Peak. 7'×12', heats to 104°F.

Cover secured at 6 points. Unlatch all straps, slide toward house — best with two people.

Equipment room under deck — pull metal handles on hatch, ladder to heat pump. Toggle to spa icon to adjust temp.

⚠ Re-cover when not in use. Cabana towels (blue & white striped) in mudroom.

🧖 Sauna & Gym

Basement gym, back-left corner. Pre-heat 30–45 min before entering. Door must be closed for heater to start.

Black controller left of door: press once to start, dial adjusts temp (right = warmer).

Functional universal trainer available for use.

🚙 Polaris Xpedition
  1. Buckle all seat belts first — speed is governed without them
  2. Press brake firmly, turn key right until dash illuminates
  3. Wait ~5 sec for dash and console to finish scrolling
  4. Rotate key fully right, hold until engine fires

⚠ Max 30 mph on ranch roads.

🍳 Kitchen
Refrigerators

Main fridge (wood paneled) · Pantry fridge (east end) · Beverage fridge (left of coffee maker)

Coffee Machine (Thermador)

Door pulls open: water reservoir, bean hopper, drip tray, milk container.

Dishwasher

Wood-paneled, right of main sink. Pods under sink. After pressing Start, confirm a blue light on the floor — if absent, cycle hasn't started.

🏔 On the Ranch
Trails & Roads

Miles of gravel roads — hike, bike, or take the Polaris. Elk, mule deer, and antelope are common. Walk up Running Bear Ln toward the Sangres for spectacular valley and Pikes Peak views.

Fishing · Shooting · Horses · Rafting

1.5-mile private Arkansas River lease — some of the best trout fishing in Colorado. Shooting range, horse riding, and river rafting available by arrangement.

Contact Andrew to coordinate with the ranch manager.

✅ Before You Leave

Tap each item to check off.

  • Unmake used beds — pile sheets on top with towels
  • Empty all perishables from refrigerators
  • Clean coffee machine drip tray and grounds container
  • Empty and clean milk container, return to fridge
  • Run the dishwasher
  • Turn thermostats down to 60°F
  • Turn plunge pool heater down to 65°F
  • Lock and deadbolt all exterior doors
